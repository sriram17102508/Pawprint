🐶 Pet Adoption Questions

The Pawprint adoption process has 4 simple steps:
1️⃣ Browse our adoption directory and find a pet you match with.
2️⃣ Fill out our fast online adoption application.
3️⃣ Our team schedules a meet & greet session with the shelter/pet.
4️⃣ Complete documentation and welcome your new friend home!
Adoption assistance through Pawprint is 100% free. No platform charges apply. Shelters might request a small care fee (₹500–₹2000) directly to cover recent vet procedures, vaccinations, or deworming, which goes entirely to the animal welfare facility.
To ensure safety, we verify the applicant's ID details, residential proof, and check if pet policies in their housing societies allow dogs. Our volunteers also conduct a friendly phone interview or a video call to assess readiness for pet parenting.
Adoption is a lifelong commitment, but we understand rare compatibility issues arise. We offer a 14-day trial period. If things don't work out, our volunteer network and behaviourists will assist in finding a better environment or transition the pet back safely.

🏥 Vet & Healthcare Questions

Yes, absolutely. 100% of the veterinarians listed on our platform are verified, licensed under state veterinary councils, and background-checked for clinical expertise. We display their credentials and review histories publicly on their profiles.
You can schedule a video call with a certified general vet or specialist through the app. Video consulting is ideal for minor skin issues, behavioural consults, diet advice, or post-surgery reviews. Prescriptions are uploaded directly to your pet's profile.
Yes, home visits are available in select cities. Vets can visit your home for routine checkups, deworming, vaccination, blood sample collection, and basic wound dressing to keep your pet stress-free in their home environment.
Pawprint runs a 24/7 priority emergency triage line. You can connect with emergency veterinary specialists instantly via video or calls, or get references to the nearest open 24/7 physical critical care clinics.

✂️ Grooming & Spa Questions

Most dogs benefit from professional grooming every 4–8 weeks depending on breed and coat type. Double-coated breeds need more frequent de-shedding sessions, while short-coated indies might only require a bath and nail trim every 6 weeks.
Yes, our groomers undergo special training in force-free handling and animal behaviour. We schedule longer, patient sessions for nervous pets, taking frequent breaks, using treats, and avoiding restraints to maintain a relaxed experience.
We use only premium, organic, pH-balanced, and cruelty-free shampoos and conditioners tailored to your dog's skin type (e.g. aloe vera, oatmeal, or organic neem). Specialized medicated baths are available upon vet recommendation.
Grooming sessions include a complimentary pickup and drop-off service. A temperature-controlled vehicle with professional handlers will safely transport your dog from your doorstep to our partner salon and return them fresh and clean.

🎓 Training & Boarding Questions

Pawprint strictly mandates CPDT-certified force-free training methods. We rely 100% on positive reinforcement (rewarding good behaviour with treats, play, and praise) and forbid choke collars, shock devices, or physical corrections.
Every boarding partner undergoes a multi-step verification process: we check home ventilation, space capacity, secure balconies, pet safety history, and conduct in-person home trials. Hosts must have dog care certification to be listed.
Yes, definitely. Hosts are required to send daily updates containing photos, video clips, weight tracks, and feeding confirmation messages through the dashboard chat at least 3 times a day.
Our dog boarding includes cage-free boarding, customizable premium diets, regular play & grooming checkups, 24/7 supervision by handlers, daily updates with photos/videos, and direct access to on-call veterinary doctors.

🛍️ Pet Shop & Orders Questions

We offer next-day delivery across all major metro cities. For other locations, standard shipping takes 2 to 4 business days. You can track your order package live via your dashboard.
Yes! You can set up recurring subscriptions (monthly or weekly) for food, treats, and hygiene essentials. Subscribers save an extra 10% on every recurring delivery and can modify schedules anytime.
We offer a hassle-free 7-day return policy for unused toys, apparel, and pet accessories in original packaging. Food items and custom medicines are not eligible for returns due to hygiene standards.
Yes. Every brand and product range we stock is scrutinized by our veterinary board for high-quality proteins, healthy ingredients, and complete absence of harmful chemical additives.

🧡 Volunteer & Support Questions

Becoming a volunteer is fast and fun! Simply visit our Volunteer page, complete the basic details and preferences, pass the 3-question dog care assessment quiz (scoring at least 2/3), and download your official digital Volunteer ID Card instantly.
Pawprint volunteers participate in rescue operations, manage local stray feeding drives, conduct home verification checks for adoption applicants, help run adoption fairs, and spread awareness about animal welfare in their neighborhoods.
Go to the "Lost & Found" section on the navigation bar, click "Report Pet", fill out the pet's details (appearance, breed, location last seen), upload photos, and select your contact info. The system will broadcast a push notification alert to all volunteers nearby.
